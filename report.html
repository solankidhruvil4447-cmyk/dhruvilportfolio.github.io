What We Learned

🌍

Geographic Restrictions Matter

Helcim’s rejection revealed they only support US and Canadian businesses — a critical filter we hadn’t considered. Future outreach must segment by regional compliance.

🔤

“Partner” Definitions Vary

Helcim defines “Partner” as “SaaS Integrator embedding our product.” We pitched as “Services Orchestrator” – immediate disqualification.

⏳

Sales Cycles Are Longer

Zero contracts closed in 60 days. Industrial services require 3‑6 months of trust‑building before commitment.

📧

Direct Contact Wins

7shifts replied with an automated message — our inquiry went to a generic contact form. Direct email to decision‑makers is non‑negotiable.

“After reviewing your application for Altrion Systems, we will not be moving forward with a formal partnership. Our Partner Program is strictly reserved for SaaS Integrators… Helcim only supports businesses in the United States and Canada.”
— Helcim Partner Team
