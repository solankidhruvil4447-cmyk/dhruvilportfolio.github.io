Case Study — 60-Day Validation Sprint

Altrion Advisors

Testing transparent vendor orchestration for B2B industrial + digital services.

Q1 2026Founder & Lead Researcher Live prototype →

The problem

Businesses needing both industrial automation (PLC panels, IoT) and digital services (web dev, support) must manage 3–5 separate vendors. This creates fragmented communication, unpredictable costs, and no single point of accountability.

Our hypothesis

A single contract with transparent line-item pricing — showing vendor cost and our fee separately — would appeal to operations leaders tired of vendor chaos.

What we built & tested

  • Landing page: 6-section responsive site on Lovable.dev showcasing service integration.
  • Pricing model: "What you pay is what you see" — vendor cost + 18% fee, fully transparent.
  • Outreach campaign: 50+ personalized cold emails to CTOs and Ops heads at B2B SaaS/industrial firms.
  • Follow-up sequence: Day 1, Day 3, Day 7 multi-touch cadence.

Key insights

Geographic restrictions

Helcim only supports US/Canada — a critical filter for our global positioning.

Partner definitions vary

"Partner" meant "SaaS integrator" to Helcim, not "services orchestrator."

Sales cycles > 60 days

Industrial trust-building requires 3–6 months; sprint length insufficient for close.
